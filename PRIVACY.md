# Privacy & consent

OurChat analyzes a group chat, which contains **other people's messages**. Please
be a good citizen.

## What OurChat does
- Reads a file **you exported yourself** from KakaoTalk (`대화 내용 내보내기`). It never
  decrypts the app database or accesses anyone's device.
- Runs **locally** by default. With no LLM configured, nothing leaves your machine.

## What leaves your machine (only if you opt in)
- **Bring-your-own LLM**: message samples/aggregates are sent to the provider *you*
  configured (OpenAI/Anthropic/your local model). Choose a local model (Ollama) for
  full locality.
- **Hosted mode**: you upload the export to the hosted service. Opt-in only.

## Please
- Get **consent** before publishing or sharing a dashboard that names real people.
- Prefer **offline mode** for sensitive rooms (family, work, private groups).
- Don't use OurChat to surveil, dox, or harass. It analyzes patterns, not private
  identities — keep it that way.
- You are responsible for complying with KakaoTalk's terms and local laws on the data
  you export and share.

OurChat is not affiliated with Kakao Corp.
